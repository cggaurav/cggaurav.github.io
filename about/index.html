About

I am Gaurav - I am a software engineer, product manager by day and build side projects that keep me up at night. I love running, being out experiencing nature, swimming in the oceans and jumping off planes.

I have a startup mindset, think global, work well in diverse & multicultural teams & ask tons of questions. I have previously lived and worked in New York, Stockholm, Singapore & Bangalore and have been around Latin America as a digital nomad for a couple of years.

I built a company called Merchii in Stockholm, Sweden which brought e-commerce and inspiration together by giving publishers a tool to tag their beautiful images with products; we sold the company to a media startup in New York in '14.

I am excited about building innovative sustainable technology companies that have a real world impact and solve a meaningful problem having a solid business model.

I love learning new things, be it dance, music, sports and travel the world to experience different cultures with a focus on listening to perspectives that I don't necessarily agree with.

I grew up in a musical family and hence play the Indian drum called Tabla in a Hindustani music context and perform once in a while.

I speak good Spanish, basic Swedish and I am learning Chinese/Pinyin.

I am adventurous - skydive, freedive, surf; I used to be a competitive swimmer as a kid; and start my day with a espresso macchiato.
