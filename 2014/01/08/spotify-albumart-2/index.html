Visualizing an Artist Album Art

The content/images that turn out as album art are creative and as a small hack, I spent some time looking at how you can get an artist top albums' art into a single unified view. With Spotify not having an open API to pull album art, I had to look up an unofficial albumArt API to pull the album Art in different sizes. Further, with a dirty CSS background-image hack, you can visualize the artwork at AlbumArt by using an Artist's Spotify URI or URL.
