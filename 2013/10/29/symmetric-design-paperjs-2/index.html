Symmetric Design | PaperJS

  • Gaurav C
  • October 29, 2013
  • Design

I have been fascinated lately by symmetric design which have a heavy touch of minimalism in them. I believe that to explore symmetry, we need to be able to see multiple perspectives of the design.

To start out, I took a Sandbox Logo and used Paper.js to animate it. All I needed to create the design was just a square, ie a square length and the angle of the parallellograms in them. Further, I rotate them to give you a feel of the symmetries that arise from such a simple thing.

This philosophy is in line with the examples that the Twitter logo can be constructed from sets of concentric circles.
